About us!

Welcome to The Herbie Pie, an Italian vegan pizza shop located in London. We are proud to offer a menu that is entirely plant-based, using locally sourced, organic ingredients. We believe in promoting sustainable living by supporting local farmers and reducing our carbon footprint. At The Herbie Pie, we are committed to providing our vegan customers with delicious and authentic Italian pizza, Our pizzas are made with traditional Italian methods and techniques, with every ingredient carefully selected and prepared to deliver the ultimate vegan pizza experience. From our homemade pizza dough to our rich tomato SatJCe and plant-based Cheese, our menu is designed to satisfy your cravings while staying true to our cruelty-free philosophy. We are also committed to fair trade ethics, working closely with our suppliers to ensure that everyone involved in our supply chain is treated with respect and fairness. We believe that promoting ethical and sustainable practices is not only good for our planet but also the right thing to do. So come and join us for a slice of The Herbie Pie! We are confident that you will love our delicious vegan pizzas and feel good knowing that you are supporting a business that cares about the environment, local communities, and fair trade ethics.
